BACKGROUND & RATIONALE

Africa's demographic dividend demands action

The African Union Students' Platform (AUSP) spans 46 African countries and global diaspora. With over 70% of sub-Saharan Africa under 30, this conference is AUSP's flagship intervention to translate potential into power — placing youth at the heart of Agenda 2063 and SDGs.

🌍

Official languages: Arabic, English, French, Portuguese, Spanish, Swahili. Sign language interpretation provided.

GLOBAL IMPACT

Reframe the narrative

Africa's success is critical to global stability. This conference produces exportable models in governance, Blue Economy, and digitalization, strengthening multilateralism between Agenda 2063 and SDGs.

INCLUSION
